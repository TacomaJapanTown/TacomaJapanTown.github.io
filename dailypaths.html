Daily Paths

This map combines information from Japanese Language School project interviews, various editions of Polk's Tacoma City Directories, and literature. You can find out more here

In the interviews, various interviewees described their daily paths on their way to public school, then the language school, and back home. In this map you can see each person's path and read details about it by clicking on their icon.
